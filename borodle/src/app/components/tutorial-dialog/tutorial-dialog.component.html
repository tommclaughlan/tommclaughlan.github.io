HOW TO PLAY

Guess the BORODLE in six tries.

Each guess must be a valid six-letter Boro player (past or present). Hit the enter button to submit.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Examples

The letter B is in the word and in the correct spot.

The letter R is in the word but in the wrong spot.

The letter I is not in the word in any spot.
